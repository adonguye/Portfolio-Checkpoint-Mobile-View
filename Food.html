Foods of the United States Vigin Islands

St. Croix --- st.Thomas --- St.John

I ate at over a 3 dozen different places during the summer.

Cafe Fresco was 9/10

$$

Vibe:Outdoor patio healthy food

Pros: Great salads, get the peanut punch if you want a sweet smoothie pick me up. Food usually is enough for two meals

Cons:They take forever and have the weirdest hours

Spicy Grill was 3.5/10

$$

Vibing: A shack, not for sitting. The spot you go when you’re hungry at 64 West and forgot lunch because its the only walkable place

Pro: Lots of food for the money

Straight up not that great. Large portions so its enough lunch for 2 days *Don’t order roti

Chicken Shack

Overall rating: 10/10

$$

A pick-up spot, has sitting but better as a grab and go.

Best roasted chicken and Johnny cakes on this island

The local ice cream places were the best. There were so many ice creams made with local fruits that I would try to get some every week. Each week I tried a new ice cream flavor.
